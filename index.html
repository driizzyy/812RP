Common Questions

What is 812RP?

We are a Serious RP community based on Jeffersonville, Indiana. We focus on immersion, realistic interactions, and community-driven stories.

Do I need a whitelist?

Currently, 812RP is in a closed beta release, so we're still squashing a few bugs. Whitelist applications for PD, EMS, and Mechanics are open in our Discord.

How do I join the PD or EMS?

Join our Discord, head to the #applications channel, and fill out the form for the department you are interested in.
